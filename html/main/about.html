Oak University Library is a library management system for the fictional Oak University. It was created as a project for a web development class.

Oak University was the first university founded on Mars, in the year 2019. Oak is the only university on Mars that offers courses to extraterrestrials. The library boasts a large selection of literature in several languages, including English, Martian, Klingon, Dothraki, and more.

Images of book covers were taken from Open Library. https://openlibrary.org/dev/docs/api/covers
